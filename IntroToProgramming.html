Introduction to Programming

Course Overview

In these two half-year courses, students will be given a powerful foundation in programming with the use of various different languages. It will serve as a base and is useful for those who want to due more difficult programming courses in the future.

Prequisites

None

Grading

Students will complete several projects of varying difficulty to assess their knowledge of the current topic.
